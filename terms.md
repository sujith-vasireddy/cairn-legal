## What Cairn is

Cairn is a private outdoor journal for iPhone. It lets you 
track routes, mark places, and share what you want with people 
you choose. It is not a navigation app, a safety service, or 
an emergency service.

## Safety disclaimer

Cairn's optional "expected return" safety net is a best-effort 
feature. It prepares a text message to your chosen contact if 
you don't end your outing by your expected return time. 
However, the message requires you or an external delivery 
mechanism to complete, and it depends on your phone having 
signal when needed.

Do not rely on Cairn as your only safety measure in remote or 
dangerous environments. Use proper safety equipment, tell 
reliable people where you're going, and consider dedicated 
safety devices (Garmin inReach, personal locator beacons, 
etc.) for true wilderness travel.

We are not responsible for any harm that results from relying 
on Cairn's safety features.
